Applications of Spectroscopy

Spectroscopy is widely used in pharmaceuticals, forensic science, nanotechnology, environmental science, biochemistry, food technology and advanced scientific research. Modern spectroscopy helps scientists identify compounds, study molecular structures and analyze materials accurately.
